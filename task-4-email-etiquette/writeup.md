Good communication is very important in professional life. Poor digital communication can lead to misunderstandings and create a negative impression.

For example, sending an email without a proper subject or greeting may seem unprofessional and can confuse the receiver. Similarly, using informal language or emojis in professional emails can reduce credibility.

To avoid such issues, it is important to follow proper email etiquette such as using clear subject lines, polite language, and structured format. This task helped me understand how to communicate effectively and professionally in digital platforms.
