# Stay Safe Online - Prevention Checklist

- Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
- Always verify payment requests before approving
- Use strong and unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
- Keep your apps and software updated
- Install antivirus and security apps
- Regularly check bank statements for unusual activity
- Report cybercrime on cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930
