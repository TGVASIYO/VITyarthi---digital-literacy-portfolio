I created my Digital Literacy Awareness infographic to help students understand the importance of using digital tools safely and effectively. The infographic covers key areas such as digital literacy, safe internet practices, and email etiquette. 

In the first section, I explained digital literacy as the ability to use digital tools, evaluate information, and communicate responsibly. The second section highlights safe internet practices like using strong passwords, avoiding suspicious links, and protecting personal information. The third section focuses on email etiquette, including writing clear subject lines, maintaining a formal tone, and using proper greetings and signatures.

I also included a bonus section to explain why digital literacy is important, such as protecting against cybercrime and improving academic performance. While designing, I focused on keeping the layout clean, colorful, and easy to understand using icons and short bullet points. 

One challenge I faced was balancing visuals and text, but I managed it by organizing content clearly. This task improved my ability to present information in a simple and engaging way.
